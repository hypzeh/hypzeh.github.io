RT_GFX


RT_GFX is a solo project piece was developed in C++ using DirectX 11, AntTweakBar & DirectXTK libraries. All model objects were created and textured by me using a small variation of modelling/creative software packages, including; Blender, Cinem4D, Photoshop, Substance Painter...etc.

The underwater scene developed consists of a few sea-life creatures, old-fashioned scuba diver and two types of submarine; modern and drebbel. All of which, are encapsulated within a semi-transparent sphere.
