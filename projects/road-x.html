ROAD-X


ROAD-X (Road Cross) is 2D road crossing game where the player takes the comical role of a chicken and overcomes the mass traffic and other terrain that is generated in front of them. This project was created and developed solo, for a dissertation in University.

With no real end to the game, the main player focus is score; the score is increased with each new progressive step forward on the treachery roads and fields. With a very familiar setting and typical arcade mechanics; the game allows anyone to jump straight in and take on the challenge of the road.

The main focus and vision of the project was (and still is) "Replay-ability"; all decisions on planning and development of the project were made with the 'Easy to learn, hard to master' set of mind. I envisioned the player to be able to pick the game up and from their first experience understand the fundamentals, allowing for further progression and replay-ability in achieving higher scores creating an competitive environment between friends and a sense of self-achievement.
