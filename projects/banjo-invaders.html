BANJO INVADERS


Banjo Invaders is a game that was assigned within my first year at University of Hull as an Assessed Coursework piece.

This assignment was my first real introduction to coding and development. The concept of this game takes inspiration from "Space Invaders", the Attacking Banjo's will spawn periodically from the top of the screen and depending on what type of Banjo it is, move down to the bottom of the playerâ€™s screen. The player controls an accordion which moves left and right along the bottom of the screen, the task is to shoot the incoming Banjo's before they reach the bottom of the screen. Score is added based on what type of Banjo is destroyed and lives are lost for every Banjo that reaches the bottom, game finishes when the player has depleted all their lives and their final score is displayed on screen.
