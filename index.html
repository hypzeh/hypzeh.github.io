ABOUT ME

Hi there, I'm Nick. I'm a 22-year old that has recently graduated as a Master of Engineering in Computer Science with a 2:1. When I am not working, personal projects tend to take over my free time, which further develops my knowledge and interests while also creating entertainment for my online following "The Pride" under my internet profile and alias "Hypzeh".

My passion for my field started from art; Most of my proudest memories of the past usually start with a pen in my hand. Although, that pen has mostly translated into a Keyboard & Mouse, the desire for creating and innovating has only got stronger. I always find myself creating or further building on what I hold close to my heart, whether it is computer hardware, software, graphics, or technology in general, the tasks relating to those topics are neither work nor hobby; they are my entire existence. To most, that would be rather sad, to me however the self-worth during an assignment and the feeling of achievement upon completion is immeasurable. Due to my commitment to any project or assignment my friends and family would often describe me as a "Perfectionist" because I would not be satisfied with project that has a pixel out of place, while it is true that I would not be satisfied until I have achieved a high standard of anything that I task myself with, (sorry, Leonardo da Vinci) I refuse to accept any standard being perfect as a whole; as stated I often find myself creating, developing and further building on projects, we as the human race are ever-growing and learning and with each day that goes past and each lesson learnt, we are able to reflect and improve. My dream is to be part of the mould that will shape the limitless potential of the future, through technology.
